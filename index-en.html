About Us


Bonyan Gostar Arvand

Bonyan Gostar Arvand is the first commercial manufacturer of HIGH PERFORMANCE PRETENSIONED SPUN CONCRETE PILE (HPC Piles) in Iran. The company incorporated in Arvand Special Zone, Iran on October 2011 and the CENTRIFUGAL PRETENSIONED SPUN PILES and POLES project commenced in November 2011 which offer an economical foundation system with consistent and superior quality compared to the ordinary concrete piles. Utilizing high technology systems and enhanced experiences in pre-tensioned concrete parts, Bonyan Gostar Arvand is improving local markets and has the potential capability to export its products to the neighbor countries as well. Our prime objective is to understand the requirements of our clients and to perform quality service for them on time and cost competitive prices.








Product

HPC Piles

Our produced Piles are being used extensively as foundation piles for power stations, high-rise buildings, civil construction, bridges, marine structures, ports, schools and government projects. These piles are easier to handle and drive because of the high strength to weight ratio and allows to wider choice of rig and hammer size.

HPC Poles

In spun-concrete pole technology, the spinning and the tensioning of the steel strands produce a dense and pore-free surface that is extremely resistant to aggressive environmental media such as air and frost, and to other influences such as fire and mechanical damage. The smooth and practically pore-free, highly compressed surface requires no special inspection, coatings, nor any additional protection against corrosion.

Our Services

Engineering Solutions

The division provides specialized services such as geotechnical studies, engineering design, development engineering review and control surveying.

Bonyan Gostar Arvand ENGINEERING SERVICES is a multidisciplinary engineering excellence center offering an optimal balance of innovation and proven civil industry experience incorporating high-technology services in engineering, operations and production fields. Thanks to this dynamic breadth and depth expertise, we are able to provide customized, high quality services to meet your project demands.

Site Investigations: Carried out in accordance with BS 5930. Mobile 4-wheel drive cable percussion, rotary rigs, dismountable low head room rigs are available. In addition, we offer window sampling and hand auguring investigations for restricted access sites.

Testing: A full range of geotechnical and chemical testing on soils plus materials testing is available.

Foundation Design: A full design and detail scheme is available for pad, strip, raft and piled foundations for all types of structures.
